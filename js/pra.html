Entry Requirements

You must complete the Introduction to Dental Nursing Course online if you are not an oversea dentist.

We also hold quarterly workshops, if you wish to have a face-to-face training session.

Workshop

We hold one day dental nursing workshop for learner to get an idea and understanding of working as a dental nurse. The workshop is practical based and would take you into a dental practice to learn about the following:

  1. How to set up a dental surgery
  2. How to carry out the decontamination process
  3. How to set up dental tray for dental treatments
  4. Understanding the basic dental instruments and equipment

At the end of the workshop, all learners would get a short multiple choice practical test and with successful completion you would get a certification.

The certificate for the workshop is valid for 12 months, and can be a great addition to your portfolio when applying for a job
